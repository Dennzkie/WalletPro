About the Project

WalletPro E-Wallet

A mobile-first digital wallet prototype designed for seamless financial transactions.

The Problem

Many Filipinos — especially students and young professionals — still rely on cash for everyday transactions, leading to inconvenience, security risks, and difficulty tracking expenses.

Existing e-wallet solutions are often complex or intimidating for first-time users, lacking a clear and simple interface that guides them through core financial tasks.

Our Solution

WalletPro is a mobile e-wallet prototype designed with simplicity and usability at its core — providing a clean, intuitive experience for:

  • 💸 Sending and receiving money instantly
  • 🔒 Securing transactions with a trusted interface
  • 📊 Tracking expenses with a built-in history log
